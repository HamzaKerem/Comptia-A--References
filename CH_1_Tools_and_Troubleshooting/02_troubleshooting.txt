Troubleshooting:

1 - Identify problem.
Question the user. Ask general questions.

2 - Establish a theory of probable cause.

3 - Test the theory to determine cause.

4 - Repeat steps 2 & 3 as neccessary.

5 - Establish a plan of action.

6 - Verify full functionality and implement preventative
meausers if neccessary.

7 - Document findings, actions, and outcomes.
